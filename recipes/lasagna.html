Lasagna Recipes

Lasagna Recipes Description

Lasagnais a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano

Ingredients

  • Dried sheets of pasta layered with rich meat ragu
  • Ricota
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles
  3. Spread an even layerof the ricotta cheese mixture
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce
  5. Repeat those layer two times
  6. Top it with the final layer of noodles, sauce, mazzarella and parmesan cheese
